OUR STORY

In 2019, we founded Sclothers to shatter the expensive, boring apparel industry. “One should not pay a fortune to acquire high-quality clothing", is the mantra of our company. Thus, giving birth to a revolution in the vibrant fashion industry of Pakistan. We no longer dress because it's a requirement, we dress to impress. Yet, the industry is plagued with overpriced, low-quality, and poorly designed apparel

MISSION

"To produce quality fabric in alignment with international standards and distribute it through an economic pricing model without compromising on style".

VISION

"To strive for international recognition of Pakistan's contribution to the global textile and fashion industry".
We believe that fashion should be fun, spontaneous, daring and shared with friends and so we built a new kind of store that Pakistan has never seen before. Rad is not only unique in its product offering but is also revolutionary in terms of operations. Our extraordinary and creative design team is constantly producing new collections. We launch collections on a weekly basis and last but not least, everything we sell is hand-produced once your order is being placed. All of our clothing line is being manufactured in our own factories located in Lahore & Faisalabad. We ship all over Pakistan using cash on delivery method.
